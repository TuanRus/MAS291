PHAN 1: XAC SUAT (Chuong 1, 2, 3)

Chuong 1 & 2: Xac suat co ban

  • Quy tac cong: P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A ∩ B)
  • Quy tac nhan: P(A ∩ B) = P(A) × P(B|A). (Neu doc lap thi nhan thang).
  • Xác suất có điều kiện: P(A|B) = P(A ∩ B) / P(B).
  • Cong thuc Bayes: Dung Cay xac suat (Tree Diagram) de giai nhanh nhat.

Chuong 3: Bien roi rac (Discrete Variables)

  • Binomial (n, p): Thu n lan, tinh so lan thanh cong x. P(X=x) = nCx * p^x * (1-p)^(n-x)
  • Poisson (λ): So lan xay ra trong khoang thoi gian/khong gian. (Chu y doi λ cung don vi). P(X=x) = (e^-λ * λ^x) / x!
  • Geometric: Thu den khi thanh cong lan dau tien tai lan thu x. P(X=x) = (1-p)^(x-1) * p
  • Hypergeometric: Lay n mau tu N (co K cai Success), tim x cai Success. P(X=x) = (KCx * (N-K)C(n-x)) / NCn
Meo bam may (Menu 6-1): De tinh Mean (μ) va Variance (σ²) cua bang phan phoi bat ky:
Nhap gia tri vao cot X, nhap xac suat vao cot Freq.
Ket qua: Lay x̄ (Mean) va σx (SD).

PHAN 2: BIEN LIEN TUC & THONG KE (Chuong 4, 5, 6)

Chuong 4: Phan phoi Chuan (Normal Distribution)

  • Tat ca dua ve chuan hoa Z: Z = (X - μ) / σ
  • Tinh Xac Suat: Dung Normal CD.
  • Tim gia tri k: Dung Inverse Normal.

Chuong 5: Thong ke mo ta

  • Sample Mean (x̄): Trung binh mau.
  • Sample SD (s): Do lech chuan mau (chia cho n-1).
LUU Y SONG CON: Khi bam may thong ke mau (Menu 6-1), ket qua do lech chuan phai lay dong sx. Tuyet doi khong lay σx.

Chuong 6: Ung dung nang cao (CLT)

Khi de hoi xac suat cho Trung binh mau (Mean) cua n phan tu:

Meo bam may sieu nhanh:
Van dung Normal CD, nhung tai o σ, nhap phep tinh: σ goc ÷ √(n).

PHAN 3: SUY DIEN THONG KE (Chuong 7 - 10)

Chuong 7 & 8: Mot mau (1 Sample)

Quy tac chon Z hay T:

  • Biet σ HOAC n ≥ 30 → Dung Z.
  • Khong biet σ (chi co s) VA n < 30 → Dung T.

Chuong 9: Hai mau (2 Samples) - Chi tiet Formula Sheet

A. So sanh 2 Trung binh (μ1 - μ2)

  • Case 1: Da biet σ1, σ2
    → Dung Z-test.
  • Case 2: Khong biet σ, gia su phuong sai BANG nhau
    → Dung T-test voi Phuong sai gop (Pooled variance - sp).
    Dau hieu: "Assuming equal variances".
  • Case 3: Khong biet σ, phuong sai KHAC nhau
    → Dung T-test voi bac tu do (df) tinh theo cong thuc dai (Welch's).
    Dau hieu: "Not assuming equal variances".

B. So sanh 2 Ty le (p1 - p2)

  • Khoang tin cay (CI): Dung rieng le p̂1 va p̂2.
  • Kiem dinh gia thuyet (Test): Phai tinh Ty le gop (Pooled Proportion - p̄).

Chuong 10: Hoi quy tuyen tinh

  • Phuong trinh: ŷ = a + bx (Tren may tinh hien thi A va B).
  • He so tuong quan r: Kiem tra do manh yeu moi quan he.
  • Kiem dinh r: Dung cong thuc t = r√(n-2) / √(1-r²).
